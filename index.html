Overview

There are many reasons to visit the Finger Lakes - gorgeous lakes, wine and culinary highlights, exceptional hiking and biking, skiing, golf in our great outdoors, and of course, our family friendly atmosphere. Whatever brought you to this Website, we are glad you found us and hope you brought your sense of adventure and wonder for the things that make Ontario County in the Finger Lakes unique.

History of the Finger Lakes

Finger Lakes region dates back hundreds of millions of years. This was an age millions of years before the emergence of dinosaurs, when the precursors of life we recognize today were evolving into their first forms: the first insects, the first trees, the first seed-bearing plants, the first ferns, and the first amphibians.

Geography of the Finger Lakes

600 Million Years Ago

Formed during the Neoproterozoic Era, the Finger Lakes began their journey as a supercontinent broke apart.

Glacial Impact

Massive glaciers carved the lakes, shaping the unique geography we admire today.

Modern Landscape

A region of beauty, agriculture, and tourism, the Finger Lakes inspire with their pristine landscapes.
