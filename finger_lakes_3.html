The History of the Finger Lakes

The Finger Lakes region dates back hundreds of millions of years. This was an age millions of years before the emergence of dinosaurs, when the precursors of life were evolving into their first forms: the first insects, the first trees, the first seed-bearing plants, the first ferns, and the first amphibians.
